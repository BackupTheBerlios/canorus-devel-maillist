Hi,

I'm just listening to a LAC 2010 presentation about the
inofficial MusicXML test suite:

http://kainhofer.com/musicxml/

The video/papers etc. can be downloaded here:
http://lac.linuxaudio.org/2010/recordings/day4_1045_A_MusicXML_Test_Suite.ogv
http://lac.linuxaudio.org/2010/download/Kainhofer_Talk_MusicXML_LAC2010_2010-05-04_Presentation.pdf
http://lac.linuxaudio.org/2010/papers/19.pdf

Apart from the usual notation stuff (including guitar tabs, precussions) it
also features tests for accordian registration and MusicXML layout (this seems
to be a new feature from version 2.0 of the format). Also it seems that
MusicXML can now be "officially" compressed (zip archive) to reduce the score
size.
