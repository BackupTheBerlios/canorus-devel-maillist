Hi,

I just noted that (mainly due to using xpdf techniques) poppler is
GPL2 only. This means we are forced to change our license to GPL2 only
as well if we integrate poppler into Canorus.

The alternative possibility would be to offer printing and preview as
an optional Canorus package that requires GPL V2 and include it as
a plugin or use a different pdf rendering engine (I myself am not aware
of any other except ghostscript not being a real alternative).
