Hi guys.

I just committed the transposition support. Now, you can
1) select some notes (or none),
2) go to Tools->Transpose,
3) a nice transpose dock widget appears on the right.
4) You can choose from transpose by key signature (major -> minor
transposition - not implemented yet), by interval (eg. major second up)
or by semitones (interval is generated out of the number of semitones)
5) Click Apply

Please try it out.
