Hi guys.

I finally implemented the midi recorder now:
1) Configure your midi-in devices (keyboard etc.)
2) Tools->Midi recorder.
3) Play something
4) Stop. The midi file is produced in /tmp/mymidifile.mid

For now, the recorded file only exists in the tmp folder, but in the
future it should get added to resources automatically everytime you
record something.
