canorus-devel at lists.berlios.de pravi:
> Hi all,
>
> Attached is another patch I wrote.
> The patch fixes a bug reproduceible by the doing the following: start
> Canorus, insert a note, window->split, window->unsplit, enjoy the
> segfault.
> The viewport cloning process includes cloning the music elements and
> the context elements, and inserting the music elements into their
> contexts. The bug made the cloned music elements go to the contexts of
> the _first_ viewport, causing random crashes and memory leaks.
>
> The patch makes the following structural changes:
> - CADrawable::clone() is no longer pure. It returns
> CADrawableMusElement::clone(0). See comments inside.
> - CADrawableMusElement::clone() is now
> CADrawableMusElement::clone(CADrawableContext* newContext = 0), and is
> pure virtual.
> - CADrawableContext::clone() is pure virtual.
> - There's a new function CAScoreViewPort::importElements() which
> combines CAScoreViewPort::importMElements() and
> CAScoreViewPort::importCElements()
>
> I think that the last two functions (importMElements() and
> importCElements()) are not needed any more and can be removed. Any ideas?
>
> -- Itay Perl (aka lkjasa on IRC)
