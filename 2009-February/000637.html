Hi Grzegorz.

Fonts should be installed to Canorus data directory /fonts. On Linux,
that's /usr/share/canorus/fonts and on Windows C:\Program
files\Canorus\fonts. There is no need to "register" it anymore - the
fonts are loaded on the fly.


Regards.
-Matevž
