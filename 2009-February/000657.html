Hi Itay,

the anti-aliasing stuff is slow, but looks nicer IMO - especially if you
use higher resolution. I'll enable it by default, but will put the "use
anti-aliased fonts" in Settings. Slurs will always be anti-aliased.

About resources: I think all resources should always be saved. When
saving to myScore.xml format, a "myScore.xml files/" directory should be
made where saving along the .xml file. And when saving to .can format,
the folder should be zipped along the file.
