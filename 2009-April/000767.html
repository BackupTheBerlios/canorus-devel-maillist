Hi guys.

I changed the source tree design:
src/core/score elements are now located in src/score
src/drawable is moved to src/layout
src/interface/engraver is moved to src/layout/layoutengine.cpp/h

The src/score are the entities which represent our score.
The src/layout are the classes which render and layout the score to the
screen/paper. In the future, we could use external library for layouting for
example.
