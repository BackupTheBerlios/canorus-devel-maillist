Hi everybody,

yes it's true, I looked in it and for me it looks that qt changed the
codecs of QTextStream is modifying 0xff when written to the midi file. I
tried every trick I could imagine with QTextStream and came to the
conclusion, that midi export needs to be written by through a QDataStream.

This means, either new export class(es) or means that the current export
classes support both QTextStream and QDataStream are needed. This
involves changes in quite a few places, so I decided to share this here
first. Maybe someone has a simple idea to do this?
