Yes, it's intended. It's a free service called CIA.vc (http://cia.vc).
It keeps some statistics (http://cia.vc/stats/project/canorus) and
allows you to track commits in different ways, e.g. by RSS, or by
putting an IRC bot in the project's channel and in #commits (also on
Freenode; #commits channel reports every single commit from any
project that is listed on cia.vc. It's quite amazing, definitely worth
a visit).
