Hi everyone,

for lilypond export I crafted a preliminary hack to have volta brackets
in the score. With special text marks you get the following:

    voltaRepeat <some Text>   bracket to the next RepeatClose or
RepeatCloseOpen
    voltaBar <some Text>         bracket to the next bar line

This is a reasonable hack, I think, until we have a proper gui for a
dedicated element. Some explanation is in the .ly file, in which we need
now the special function voltaStart. Comments are welcome, and if this
hack has a chance of a longer life I could also describe it in our manual.

It's only, because I need volta brackets quite often. One lilypond
warning I couln't get rid of, still looking into this.
Best Regards,
