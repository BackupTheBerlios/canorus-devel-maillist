Hi Orcan.

Canorus is currently licensed under GPL v2, because it depended on Qt4 
which was licensed under QPL/GPLv2 only (until half a year ago). We 
would like to relicense Canorus to GPL v3, but we haven't decided yet - 
that's why no specific GPL version is specified in the source files.

We contain two external snippets: RtMidi (free compatible license, see 
http://www.music.mcgill.ca/~gary/rtmidi for details) and pmidi (GPL v2).

Examples: .can files are licensed some under GPL and some under Creative 
Commons share-alike or public domain (see specific example for details - 
they MUST be distributed along), MusicXml samples are copyrighted by 
Recordare (see http://www.recordare.com/xml/samples.html) - I'm not 
sure, if they can be freely distributed, Midi files are downloaded all 
over the internet and probably can't be distributed along.

I renamed the LICENSE.GPL to COPYING in R1174 as it's the GNU naming 
standard for this.


Thanks for your effort on packaging!
-Matevž
