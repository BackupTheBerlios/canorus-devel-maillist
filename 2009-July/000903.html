Hi Matevž,

We have a list of "good" content licenses:
http://fedoraproject.org/wiki/Licensing#Content_Licenses
and a list of "good" documentation licenses:
http://fedoraproject.org/wiki/Licensing#Documentation_Licenses

Note that here "good" means: "allowed in Fedora". Some of them are
also declared "free" by FSF. The above links also tell you which
licenses also FSF free. FSF free licenses are most probably "good" in
all Linux distributions.

Cheers,
Orcan
