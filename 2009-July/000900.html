Hi,

Under this circumstances yes, only files with clear license
might be a kind of non-free add-on package.

I feel uneasy about this concerning the fact that it might
be problematic to host files in our repository with nonfree
license but that's a different topic.

My contributions (if someone uploaded one) are all published
under Creative Commons Attribution Share-Alike 3.0 germany.

Every contributor has to make it's own statement for his
files (IMO we should remove them if they fall under the non
free category).

If you wish we can add some note that all examples files uploaded
are automatically assumed to be contributed under the Creative
Commons CA-BY (author will be listed in our AUTHORS file included
with every Canorus release). NC/ND contributions are considered as
nonfree and cannot be included in Canorus releases.

Regards,

Reinhard
