Hi,
I'm packaging canorus for Fedora. I have a couple issues I'd like to
discuss. As I was told on IRC freenode_#canorus, I'm submitting these
to this mailing list.

The first issue is the license.

* The LICENSE.GPL file contains the text of GPL version 2.
* Most source files say that they are "Licensed under the GNU GENERAL
PUBLIC LICENSE", but they do not specify a GPL version. As the clause
9 of GPL version 2 license text indicates, if the source file does not
specify a GPL version, it can be distributed under any version of GPL.
So to make things clear: is canorus GPL (v2 only) or GPL (v2 or later)
or GPL (any version)?
* Most source files refer to a non-existing COPYING file. Typo?
* Some sources, such as src/import/pmidi/except.c say that they are
GPL (v2 only) which add to my confusion.

Other than these, there are content files, such as midi files or
musicxml files. What license(s) do these files have? I may need to
strip these files from the Fedora package if they are not released
under a free license.

Thanks for your help!
