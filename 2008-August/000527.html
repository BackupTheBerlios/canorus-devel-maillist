Hi guys.

I finally implemented the tuplets. To create a tuplet:
1) Either pick a note length and enable the tuplet and place the
note/rest (rests needed to fill the tuplet length are appended) or
2) Select already placed notes and rests and create a tuplet.

Saving/Loading works and LilyPond export is supported.
