Dear David,

thanks a lot for your article "Interface Design in Canorus: Reducing Modes". 
Although I do not agree with you concerning the issue on ties and slurs I 
think it's important to ponder on the question of modes in computer programs 
in general and score writers in particular. I would like to recommend you 
and every other reader of this list to try out Igor Engraver which has many 
of the features you are proposing under the section "Don't make me leave 
note entry". Igor Engraver is deplorably an example of "abandonware" with a 
company management that doesn't seem to care about their product. The owners 
of the company Noteheads AB has also shown interest in selling it.
