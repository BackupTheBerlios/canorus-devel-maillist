Developers,

Here's a new list of bugs:

- By convention stems should point down when a (single) note is on the 
center line. In Canorus stems incorrectly point up in automatic mode.

- Key signatures of 7 sharps (or flats) become 7 double sharps (or 
double flats) on LilyPad export, i.e. "\key cisis \major" instead of 
"\key cis \major" (or "\key ceses \major" instead of "\key ces \major")

- Octave placement of sharps and flats in key signature should follow 
convention. Canorus breaks this convention (for example Bb in bass cleff 
should be on the lower octave).

- Clicking on existing notes with note tool causes a stall--no other 
notes can be added to the score until you switch tools.
