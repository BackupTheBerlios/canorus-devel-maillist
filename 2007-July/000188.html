Developers:

I have written up some thoughts on improving usability in Canorus. I 
have been reading up on usability lately and given quite a bit of 
thought to improving usability in Canorus. I do not claim to be a 
usability guru but wanted to share my thoughts with the group. The 
thoughts and suggestions in the article are based on my personal 
experience with notation editors, casual observation of other users, and 
the simple principle of reducing unnecessary modes.

I recognizes that some of my suggestions may be disputed, but my 
intention is outline some basic usability guidance and offer specific 
suggestions. I believe a discussion of usability is important in any 
software and this article is my contribution to that discussion. Canorus 
has its share of problems but it is not alone in the world of notation 
software. The suggestions outline in the article could apply to any of 
the notation editors on the market. With a commitment to usability I 
believe we can achieve some wonderful things at Canorus.

Interface Design in Canorus: Reducing Modes
http://davidbolton.info/articles/notation_interface_design.html

I will cross-posted this message on the user list so they can contribute 
to the discussion.
