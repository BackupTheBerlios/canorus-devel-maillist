Hello Adam,

I'm glad you're showing the interest of our score editing application.
The development team has just returned from the Linux Audio Conference
2007 in Berlin. We discussed many important features and were talking
about the future of Canorus. One of the key features is the modern
composition. Different aspects were introduced by various composers in
Berlin and the need for inputing custom vector symbols is one of the
most important ones.

If you have any ideas you would like to share about the design of the
modern score editor for contemperament music, you're welcome to speak
up. Meanwhile, I can create a Wiki account for you and you might put
your ideas there.


Best wishes.
- Matevž
