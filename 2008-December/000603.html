I think Canorus at least needs the ability to horizontally scroll past 
the last note of the score by one screenwidth. Otherwise you have to 
scroll after every single new note since the notes appear beyond the 
width of the window.

In the past I have avoided this problem by entering the notes backwards: 
starting at the end of a piece and working toward the beginning.
