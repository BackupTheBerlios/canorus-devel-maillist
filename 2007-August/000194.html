Hi guys.

I just implemented the auto-recovery feature. Canorus now saves your
opened documents every minute (or whatever interval set in settings
dialog) and when started, searches for any not-cleaned up documents.
This gives the ability to recover any documents lost if Canorus crashed.

See a screenshot:
https://canorus.berlios.de/wiki/images/a/a2/Recovery.png

Another feature added for 0.4 :)
