Hi Halil,

I'm sure Canorus is extensible enough to implement Maqam music. Do you
have any specifications how the Maqam looks like etc.

Currently, we have a score which contains voices which contain music
elements. Music elements can be playable (length > 0) or non-playable
(bars, keys, clefs etc.). Playable elements are currently notes and
rests. Probably what you need to do is to add another kind of a playable
object probably with different intonation and look.

You can look at our API:
http://svn.berlios.de/wsvn/canorus/trunk/src/core/note.h
http://svn.berlios.de/wsvn/canorus/trunk/src/core/diatonicpitch.h
http://svn.berlios.de/wsvn/canorus/trunk/src/core/playablelength.h
http://svn.berlios.de/wsvn/canorus/trunk/src/drawable/drawablenote.h


Regards & Good luck!
