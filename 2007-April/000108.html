Hello,

I am Marijn Kruisselbrink, a Google Summer of Code student, 
participating for KDE. As part of this program, I will be implementing 
music notation support for KOffice2. As both KOffice2 and Canorus use 
QT4, I thought it should be possible to join efforts and re-use large 
parts of code.
Considering the reasonably clean design of Canorus, I think it should 
not be too dificult to seperate re-usable parts of the code in a 
separate library, allowing other applications to benefit from it. I'm 
especially thinking of the model and view parts of Canorus (perhaps even 
in separate libraries).
I think such a separation would not only benefit other projects but 
could also be benficiary for Canorus, as more people using your code 
would mean that bugs are spotted sooner, and could perhaps even result 
in more people contributing to Canorus.

So, what do you guys think of this?

Marijn
