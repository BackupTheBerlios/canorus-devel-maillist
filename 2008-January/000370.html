Hi Stefan,

Rosegarden is a sequencer, not a music score editing application. It
works only on MIDI events and is meant for reproducing, mixing etc.
music. Canorus is a score editing application similar to Finale or
Sibelius under Windows.

Canorus is also a cross-platform application and more light-weight than
Rosegarden.
