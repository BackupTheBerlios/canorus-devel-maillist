Hi everybody,

in rev686/687 I did a limit on the number of dots allowed when inserting
a note. The dots are limited, so that the flags and dots of a note
together will not make the notes resolution smaller then a 64th note.

I also did a reset on dots when changing the insertion note length, that
is, when you press a number key. I hope you find this behaviour an
improvement. Otherwise, please complain. I thought that in most cases,
when a note length changes, the number of dots changes too.

Gui-related: I tried to show the shadow notes dots to be inserted
visible. It now works if you move the mouse. Maybe someone could add the
trick to show them also right after the inserting mouse click.


Regards,
