current English names in Canorus 0.5 Beta? And then some reflections: The
tool "Insert articulation mark" contains playing technique marks and
ornaments; these, I think, should have their own buttons on the toolbar.
The "Insert slur" tool contains the tie which should have its own tool.
One could even suggest that the phrase mark should have its own tool. An
alternative would be to rename the current tool "Insert slur" to "Curved
lines" and keep the tie, the slur and the phrase mark there. The stems
for half notes to sixteenth notes should be 3.5 spaces long; for
thirty-seconds 4.5 spaces, for sixty-fourth notes 5.5, and so on.
