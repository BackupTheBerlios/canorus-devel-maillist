When you talk about redesign, especially related to performance... these
are things I thought about the last days. Two conclusions I would like
to share:

-- run up/down algorythms over the voices should, instead of linearly
stepping from element to element, better do binary searches. To look up
a specific timeStart() element would be a candidate for this, this would
be faster, I guess.
It could be done already now, it's only a change inside a function, This
would work on elements that are evenly distributed over the voice/staff,
for example notes, rests, barlines.

-- rare elements, usually time and key signatures, repeats, should be
kept in seperate or additional lists, as linked lists to the
voice/staff/sheet. They could live in the staff class. The burden to
keep them up do date is a significant change, regarding the current
mechanics. At midi import I'm currently implementing local signature
lists, that once should move over to what you mention above.
