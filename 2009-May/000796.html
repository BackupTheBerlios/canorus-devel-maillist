Please, for god's sake, do take this book's recommendations with a
huge grain of salt. Theoretical beauty of many patterns is greatly
exaggerated, and gradually I see more and more good libraries spoiled
by blatant "let's do everything as the book says".

Same thing goes about some other "sacred" OOP literature, such as
Alexandrescu or Abrahams.
