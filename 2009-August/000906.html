Hallo Reinhard,

according to your lily snippet I did the following: When the score has
no time signature and no barline then the default engravers for time
signature and barlines are switched off. This may sometimes be not
sufficient when you want no bars, but somewhere a barline.

And here comes the trick: When you do a score only with hidden rests,
then you can do this score with more or less empty staffs. But be
warned, copying hidden rests is like writing ghost music :)
Supressing the bar number then should be done too. You can do an
empty-music-page.can file now.
