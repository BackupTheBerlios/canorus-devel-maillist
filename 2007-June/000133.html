Is there a page on the wiki dedicated to the shortcuts used in canorus?
I'm a bit slow, and until now, I just opened the xml source and changed
the values of the accidents (sharp or bemol) according to my will... no
kidding :)

Maybe it wasn't clear because I'm using a french azerty keyboard on a
laptop, without any numpad. Here is the list I figured out:

+ = sharp (hit twice, you get a double sharp) [hint: without a keypad,
use the shift key accordingly]
- = bemol
e = hide the left panel
i = show left panel
esc = change the mode (is there a name for each mode?)
b = insert bar
. = . (hit many times to grow the dot)
right click = silence

Are there more that I didn't find?
