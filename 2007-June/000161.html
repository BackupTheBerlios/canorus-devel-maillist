I advice you to translate the SVN trunk as your translation will get
into 0.4 and there are quite some new expressions in comparison to 0.3.1
already.

First, add your two-digit country and language file to
src/CMakeLists.txt around line 210 to Canorus_Translation_Srcs and
Canorus_Translation_Bins.
Rebuild the application - this will generate an empty translation file
in src/lang for the first time.
Translate the file with Qt Linguist.
Recompile the application (.ts are compiled into .qm) and check your
results.
When done, send us your .ts file.


Regards.
- Matevž
