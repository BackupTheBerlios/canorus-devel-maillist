I moved Canorus SVN to SourceForge now. Details on how to checkout the 
latest trunk are here:
https://sourceforge.net/scm/?type=svn&group_id=174904

The berlios SVN is now disabled so we don't commit anything to it by 
mistake.

I also moved the wiki website and the files from berlios to SF so the 
important parts are migrated now. I might have missed some wiki pages 
(doing it manually..) so you're welcome to check out the contents and 
upload any missing pages, images or other material from the old site. 
canorus.org domain settings have also been updated to redirect to the 
new page.

For the mailing lists
canorus-devel at lists.sourceforge.net
and
canorus-user at lists.sourceforge.net
have been created to reflect the berlios mailing lists.


Regards.
-Matevž
