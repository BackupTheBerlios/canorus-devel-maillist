The key signatures are closer, but still not quite right. The A-sharp 
and B-sharp for bass clef should be an octave lower (forming the same 
pattern as the sharps in treble clef). Likewise the A-sharp for alto 
clef should be an octave lower.

I'm not sure if there is a standard placement for sharps and flats on 
the more obscure clefs. Finale and Sibelius actually show different 
results for F-baritone, C-baritone and french viola clefs (see 
attached). Probably we should match whatever LilyPond does.
