Hi Björn,

Could you be more specific about your operating system, Qt version and
Eclipse trouble?
Canorus is built around Qt4 library. No gtk is needed nor used at all.

Font for notes, clefs and other music symbols is named (as mentioned by
Itay) Emmentaler, found in src/fonts. You have to copy it to font
directory depending on your system. Usually, system truetype fonts can
be found in /usr/share/fonts/truetype on Linux and C:\Windows\Fonts on
Windows.
