Dear Dag,

I am the manager and owner of NORDISC Music & Text in Copenhagen. Our company was established in 1983, specializing in music teaching books, music theatre and records. In 1986, we made one of the first Scandinavian DOS note programs. After a long pause, we released a comprehensive collection of templates and examples for the LilyPond language this spring. When we discovered Canorus, we decided to volunteer for the project. NORDISC is actively supporting free software, music and books, although we of course also produce a lot of commercial books and records.

Our Music Theory from 1984 has been updated regularly, and the latest version includes the full line of LilyPond functions. I will be translating the theory book into Danish in December, and I guess we can have much fun comparing our  almost identical languages and the musical terms.

Let's keep in touch. If you send me your postal address, I will send you the theory book in Danish, as soon as I finish the translation.

Best regards
