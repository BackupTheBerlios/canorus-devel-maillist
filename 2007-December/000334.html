This is not a bug, but a feature :)

The idea behind multiple helper notes is that it shows you the pitch in
other staffs too. This is useful when composing a polyphonic music so
you can directly see the relation between the note you are about to
place and the other notes at the same location in other staffs.

The current implementation is not perfect though. The helper notes
should be:
1) limited not to show the extreme pitches (eg. 10 ledger lines in other
staff if they have bass or bass_8 clef)
2) ability to turn it off in view menu
