It is a zip containing the application bundle. Usually on a mac a user  
downloads the zip of a program, double clicks to unpack it, deletes  
the zip, and launches the recently uncompressed app.

There's no installation. DMG files are special packages where you can  
add special graphics to finder's backgrounds, license popups and stuff  
like that. Even so, people still zip dmg files since they are just  
like an iso image without compression.
