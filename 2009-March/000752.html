Greetings Canorus developers,

Congratulations on the release of version 0.7!  It is good to see all  
your hard work paying off.

I'm writing to inquire about the possibility of future support in  
Canorus for alternative music notations that use a chromatic staff.   
See the Music Notation Project website for more about such notation  
systems (http://musicnotation.org/).

I realize this is not a standard feature request and would fall  
outside of the regular development goals for Canorus.  We've been  
working on adding such support to Lilypond (see http://musicnotation.org/software/) 
, and we are also interested in someday, eventually, adapting an open- 
source graphical editor app like Canorus for use with chromatic staff  
notations as well.

Is this something that could be done through Canorus' plug-in  
architecture?  Or would it require more radical tinkering with the  
source code?  I would be curious to hear your thoughts on this.

Thank you for all your work on Canorus!

Paul Morris
Board Member and Webmaster

http://musicnotation.org
