Yes that is correct, it is HTML editor now. The reason for that is
because once the student proposals period starts, student will have
your "Application Template" in his Student Proposal Content field
already pasted in. You can always just put the nice description and
the link in there, something like: "Our application template is
available at the given link..." and this will show up in his proposal
content field.

Does that answer your question ?
