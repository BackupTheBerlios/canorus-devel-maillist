The Canorus team always seeks for new developer, thank your for
your interest! I just forgot to do any updates on that offer,
Currently we are heading towards the next 0.7 release but there
are many things still to be done.

In case you didn't follow the mailing list:
- Roadmap and ToDo List on the wiki should be updated (or in the
process to be updated) to reflect the current development state
- For the Google Summer of Code we are searching for Mentors and

   so we update our current GSOC pages to reach that goal

   (see lac.linuxaudio.org/2009 for details about the conference)
   and do a workshop for both interested users and developers

and discussion about all kind of Canorus related stuff.
