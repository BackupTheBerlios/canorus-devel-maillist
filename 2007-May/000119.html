Hi,
I found a few issues and fixed them in trunk r484. I did it in a rush so I
hope it's alright.
Here's a list of a few more issues I noticed and don't have the time to fix.
Most of these are not of high priority, but if you have time, please look
into them:

1. Add many notes; Window->New view; horizontal scrollbar is not in place.
2. When inserting an empty keysig where an empty keysig is active, it
creates a small gap. In other words: run canorus; click the keysig
menutoolbutton, click on a staff a few times and then add a note. You'll see
the gap.
3. If you click a muselement while in InsertMode, you get into SelectMode,
although the insertion menutoolbar is still selected, and, if you were
inserting a note, the shadow notes are still there.
4. CRASH: Insert a MusElement to a staff, and then to a lyrics or function
marking context.
5. CRASH: Insert a note to a staff; Give this staff a lyrics context. Click
the lyrics context, underneath the note so you can type; Type something;
click outside the textbox on the yellow background, so it loses focus, but
doesn't disappear; now give it the focus again by clicking it and press
return. Ugh...
6. CRASH: run "./canorus canorus"...
