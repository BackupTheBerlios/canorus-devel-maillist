Hi guys.

I implemented today the last missing feature - slurs and phrasing slurs.
I'm thinking about releasing the major 0.3.0 in a week or so after
stabilizing a bit.

Major features implemented in 0.3.0 users might notice:
- lyrics support
- ties, slurs and phrasing slurs
- basic creation of function markings
- multiple selection
- SVG icons
- current voice coloring
- LilyPond improvements

Smaller features and bugs to be fixed till 0.3.0:
- slurs are not drawn correctly yet
- slurs and phrasing slurs support in Lily
- multiple selection of slurs is sometimes annoying
- repeat bars don't play back properly yet
- tied notes shouldn't be re-played in playback
- slurs and phrasing slurs for chords should be solved


Regards!
-Matevž
